Driven by passion, powered by innovation – our team is your partner in IELTS success!

We are a dynamic group of university students from the University of Science - VNU, united by a shared passion for innovation and education. We’ve come together to revolutionize the way learners prepare for the IELTS exam. As students ourselves, we understand the challenges of mastering a complex skill set under time pressure. This inspired us to create a solution that transforms traditional study routines into an engaging and enjoyable experience.

Our journey began with a vision: to make IELTS preparation accessible, effective, and fun for learners in Vietnam. Combining our academic knowledge with cutting-edge technology, we’ve developed a gamified learning platform that blends interactivity with personalized strategies. By turning lessons into challenges and achievements, we aim to keep learners motivated and invested in their progress.

What sets us apart is our commitment to empathy and innovation. As students, we relate to the struggles faced by test-takers, and as innovators, we strive to provide tools that address those needs. Our goal is to empower learners to achieve their IELTS goals confidently while enjoying the process. Together, we’re redefining IELTS preparation, one level-up at a time!
